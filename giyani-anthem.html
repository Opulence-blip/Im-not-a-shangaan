National Anthem of Giyani

"Giyani" is one of the compositions by Dr. Shalati Joseph Khosa (SJ Khosa). It features on the album Matimba Ya Vuyimbeleri ("The Power of Music and Singing"). This album includes various pieces centered on themes of culture, tradition, unity, and history.

The below video showcases the University of Limpopo Choristers performing Giyani, accompanied by the KwaZulu-Natal Philharmonic Orchestra

“Giyani” became the Gazankulu anthem because it was a powerful cultural composition by SJ Khosa that celebrated Tsonga pride. When Gazankulu was formed as a homeland, the song was naturally adopted as its national anthem.
