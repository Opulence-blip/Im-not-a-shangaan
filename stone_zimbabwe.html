Shangaan Amethyst

The term "Shangaan Amethyst" was created by Nharho in collaboration with Hlengwe descendant Chief, Gezani.

Chief Gezani provided written authorization, allowing us to market on their behalf, as shown in the letter below.

These rare and unique gemstone discovered in the Chibuku Mine, located in the Gezani Communal Land of Mateke, Zimbabwe.



Shangaan Amethyst is a distinctive blend of amethyst, clear quartz, and smoky quartz. It often contains red hematite inclusions and can exhibit various formations, including skeletal, elestial, and scepter shapes. Due to its formation process, it is also more likely to contain enhydros (water inclusions)





Beyond their aesthetic appeal, Shangaan Amethysts are valued in metaphysical practices. They are believed to possess healing properties, such as promoting clarity, enhancing intuition, and providing stress relief. The inclusion of red hematite is thought to ground and stabilize energy, making these crystals sought after by collectors and spiritual practitioners alike
