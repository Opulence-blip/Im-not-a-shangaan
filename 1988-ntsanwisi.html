Januaty 4, 1988

On January 4, 1988, the International Policy Institute organized a special discussion on the future of South Africa. The meeting took place at the residence of the South African ambassador, where leading academics, diplomats, and policymakers came together to explore the different paths the country might follow during a time of political and social change.

Participants in the discussion included

  • John Ebiasah – Professor, George Mason University School of Law
  • Harley Hinrichs – President
  • Alan Keyes – U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations
  • Piet Koornhof – South African Ambassador to the United States
  • Hudson Ntsanwisi – Chief Minister of Gazankulu, South Africa
  • James Robinson – Chair, International Policy Institute
  • Horace Van Rensburg – Counselor, South African Embassy
