Njhakanjhaka 2017 Court Case

This one might seem also interesting to read

This case involves a dispute over the rightful succession to the Vatsonga traditional leadership, where Risimati Wilson Mkhari claimed the position was wrongfully taken from the first house of Nwa'Xikhundu I (his lineage) and given to the second house. The court found the Provincial Committee's investigation flawed for relying on unverified oral testimony and failing to properly examine historical records, rendering the Premier's subsequent decision invalid. The court set aside both decisions and ordered the formation of an independent panel to reinvestigate the claim properly, ensuring a fair and lawful resolution to this traditional leadership dispute.
